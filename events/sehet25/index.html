Eighth Workshop on Strategies for Enhancing HPC Education and Training (SEHET25)

scheduleMonday, July 21, 2025 at 9:00 AM EDT
location_onPEARC25 Conference. Columbus, Ohio, USA

The SEHET25 workshop is an ACM SIGHPC Education Chapter coordinated effort aimed at fostering collaborations among the practitioners from traditional and emerging fields to explore strategies to enhance computational, data-enabled and HPC educational needs. Attendees will discuss approaches for developing and deploying HPC training and education, as well as identifying new challenges and opportunities for keeping pace with the rapid pace of technological advances - from collaborative and online learning tools to new HPC platforms.
